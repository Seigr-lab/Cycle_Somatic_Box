### **2.2 Organic Matter Management (for MFC Optimization)**

✅ **Multi-Zone Composting Strategy**:

**Top Zone (Aerobic)** - Cathode Optimization:
- Add **oxygen-rich organic matter** (dry leaves, paper, cardboard)
- Maintain **loose, aerated structure** for optimal cathode performance
- Monitor **oxygen penetration** and adjust ventilation as needed

**Middle Zone (Transition)** - Voltage Gradient Enhancement:
- Add **mixed organic matter** creating natural pH and ionic gradients
- Maintain **moderate moisture levels** (50-60%) for optimal transition conditions
- Balance **carbon/nitrogen ratios** for stable decomposition

**Deep Zone (Anaerobic)** - Anode Optimization:
- Add **high-moisture, nitrogen-rich materials** (food scraps, green matter)
- Maintain **compacted, oxygen-depleted conditions** for optimal anode performance
- Monitor **anaerobic activity** through biogas production indicators

### **2.3 Thermal System Optimization**

✅ **Temperature Differential Management**:

**Internal Heat Optimization**:
- Monitor **soil temperature** throughout all zones
- Ensure **thermal conduits** are distributing heat effectively to Peltier hot sides
- Adjust **insulation** to maintain optimal internal temperature (10-15°C)

**External Cold Optimization**:
- Check **heat sink performance** on all 4 exterior Peltier cold sides
- Clear any **obstructions** blocking natural convection cooling
- In winter: **maximize cold exposure** for peak thermal harvesting
